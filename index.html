Who am I?

I have a Ph.D. in Technical Communication and a Master's degree in Information Design from the Illinois Institute of Technology.

I have been teaching undergrad/grad courses in web design, writing, editing, and other communication-related topics for over a decade. I have a real passion for creating beautiful things for the web.

Contact Me

Why choose me?

Why? Because my primary focus is YOU. I utilize a user-centered design approach, which simply means that the end user experience drives a design-- and not the designer.

More importantly, I love helping people! Seriously, I do. I love explaining the process of web design and translating "tech-y" details in a way that helps you understand how your site impacts your business.
