Who am I?

My name is Chris Lam, and I'm an academic by training. My educational background is diverse, but it provides me with unique experiences that influence my work as a web designer.

I have a Ph.D. in Technical Communication and a Master's degree in Information Design from the Illinois Institute of Technology. My undergraduate degree is in history with a minor in secondary education.

I have been teaching college level courses on writing and editing, web design and development, and other technical communication courses going on a decade. While I love academic work, I have a real passion for creating things.

Contact Me

Why choose me?

Why? Because my primary focus is YOU. I take a user-centered design approach in my projects. Simply put, the end user experience and interaction drives my designs and not the designer.

A beautiful design can only take you so far. With a background in technical communication, I bring a data-driven approach to web design in all of my work.

Finally, and most importantly, I love helping people. Really, I do. I love explaining the process of web design and translating those "tech-y" details in a way that is useful for you.
