What's special about me?

Well nothing, really--except for the fact that my primary focus is YOU. I use a user-centered design approach in my projects. Simply put, user experience and interaction drives design, and not the designer.

A beautiful design can only take you so far. With my background in technical communication, I bring a unique artistic and analytical approach to web design in all of my work.

Most importantly, I love helping people. Really, I do. I love explaining the process of web design to my clients. I love answering questions and translating those "tech-y" details in a way that is useful for you.

Who am I?

My name is Chris Lam, and I'm an academic by training. I have a Ph.D. in Technical Communication and a Master's degree in Information Design from the Illinois Institute of Technology.

I have been teaching writing for the web, web design, and other digital writing courses going on a decade. While I love academic work, my true passion is making things.
